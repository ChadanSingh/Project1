    Instructions and Rules

    Instructions

  • All the disks are stacked on the left rod.

  • The objective is to move this entier stack to the right rod.

  • To make a move, choose a disk from the top of the stack left rod and move it to the right rod.

  • Keep moving disks back and forth between the rods, always following the rules, until you have successfully moved the entire stack to the destination rod.

    • Rules

  • You can only move one disk at a time.

  • A disk can be placed on top of a larger disk or an empty rod.

  • Never place a larger disk on top of a smaller one.
