How to Play

Instructions

In the Tower of Hanoi puzzle, your objective is to move a stack of disks from one rod to another while following two rules: you can only move one disk at a time, and you cannot place a larger disk on top of a smaller one. Your goal is to accomplish this task using the fewest possible moves.

Rules

  • Different-sized disks are used, with the largest disk at the bottom and the smallest at the top.
  • The goal is to move the entire stack of disks from one starting rode to the finish rod.
  • Only one disk can be moved at a time; moving multiple disks together is not allowed.
  • The objective is to solve the puzzle using the fewest possible moves. The minimum number of moves needed to solve the Tower of Hanoi.
